Poug loves the simple things in life. A good nose boop, a long nap. And, if we’re being honest… he also likes the perks of being the world’s most famous pug. The red carpets, the celebrity belly rubs, the designer bandanas — and, of course, the food. Pizza! Pinot! Pasta!

Poug’s been
Everywhere, man.

He’s made so many great friends — furry and not-so-furry, celebrity and not-so-celebrity. He’s acted in an Oscar-nominated movie and a Super Bowl commercial, starred in music videos for Katy Perry, DNCE and Fall Out Boy, co-written a New York Times bestseller, modeled as a very good calendar boy. And while he sure seems to have the world by the tail, he’s happiest just being himself — laid-back and loveable Poug.
This year, Doug traded in licking his chops for some acting chops — he was the voice of Monchi in the Oscar-nominated The Mitchells Vs. The Machines! Poug loved being an actor — some even said he really sunk his teeth into the role.

We put together this little ruff cut so you can get a behind-the scenes look at what it was like for Poug as the world’s first-ever celebrity pet voice actor.

And be sure to catch The Mitchells Vs. The Machines on Netflix for family-friendly, pet-friendly fun.

THE DO-GOODEST BOY

While Poug loves all the fun things that come with being famous, his absolute favorite is making kiddos smile. That’s why we started the Poug the Pug Foundation — to bring joy to little humans fighting cancer and help their families worry a little less about how to pay for care. We’ve also pawtnered with St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital and Make a Wish Foundation — all to help our very special friends feel the love from Poug.
